G T 3 R S

For the Real Car Guys

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS is an exhilarating, track-focused marvel, embodying the essence of precision and performance. Powered by a high-revving, naturally aspirated 4.0-liter flat-six engine, it unleashes 520 horsepower to the rear wheels through a lightning-quick seven-speed PDK transmission. Boasting a distinctive aerodynamic design, the GT3 RS commands attention with its wide body, rear wing, and aggressive stance. Stripped of excess weight, it features a purpose-built interior with racing-inspired elements. This road-legal racer, a masterpiece of engineering, offers an unparalleled driving experience, thrilling enthusiasts with its razor-sharp handling, responsive steering, and a symphony of exhaust notes.
